How to buy

1- Choose the product you need and then click on "Buy now" or "Add to cart", that will add it to your Shopping Cart.

2- Once on the purchase page you will have to follow the steps one by one, first we ask you to register, then it shows the summary of what you are going to buy, the delivery method and finally the payment method.

Shipping

1- We ship nationwide, no matter where you are or you can pick up at our branch 24 hours after payment is made.

2- Each product has an assigned size and therefore a shipping cost, which varies depending on where you are.

3- Once your purchase has been made, you will be able to view the status of your shipment in your user profile to track it.

Payment

1- Cash or debit: If you are going to withdraw for the business or requested delivery by motorcycle, you can pay directly in cash. By paying in this way you will have the promotional price indicated on the website.

2- Transfer or Bank Deposit: When you make your purchase you will receive the instructions to do so.

3- Payment with QR: only with money in account. Accreditation is immediate.
